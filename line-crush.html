The Rules

HOW TO
PLAY.

1
🟩
Pick a Piece
Three block pieces are shown at the bottom. Choose one and drag it onto the 8×8 grid wherever it fits.
2
📐
Place It Smart
Think ahead. Position pieces to fill complete rows or columns. Partial lines score nothing.
3
💥
Clear Lines
Fill a complete row or column and it clears instantly. Clear multiple at once for a combo bonus.
4
🏆
Beat Your Score
Game ends when no piece fits on the board. Your score is your legacy. Can you top it?
